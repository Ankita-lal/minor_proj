Pigeon Pea

Pigeon pea, also known as "arhar dal" in India, is a highly nutritious legume. It is a rich source of protein, dietary fiber, and various vitamins and minerals. Pigeon pea is commonly used in Indian cuisine, especially in dishes like dal, soups, and curries.

This versatile legume is also used in traditional medicine for its health benefits. It is believed to aid in digestion, boost immunity, and improve heart health. Pigeon pea plants are drought-tolerant and are often grown in arid regions, making them an important crop for food security.
